Steam Games Popularity ML

Khoi Ngo

Currently: In Progress

Using my knowledge from school, I am exploring a topic I am interested in. With the rise of pc gaming, Steam is one of the world's largest hub for video game distribution and people can purchase plethora of different types of games. In this project, I will find the top games from Steam by highest average players on at the same time per month and project where their popularity trend. This project was inspired by a Kaggle dataset provided by Bogacz which includes information  of more than 1000 games on Steam [1]. The only restrictions are the data has over 1000 games and was collected with limited entries. I will use his data to figure out the popular games I want to investigate then Bogcaz linked to the source where he collected his data. I will webscrape this website to get more data for the games I am investigating. The website is a valuable tool to measure trends of games on Steam by collecting number of concurrent players per game in the Steam catalogue [2].

References

[1] M. Bogacz, "Popularity of games on steam", Kaggle.com, 2021. [Online]. Available: https://www.kaggle.com/michau96/popularity-of-games-on-steam. [Accessed: 29- Apr- 2021].

[2] J. Gray, “Steam charts - tracking what's played,” Jul-2012. [Online]. Available: https://steamcharts.com/. [Accessed: 04-May-2021].
